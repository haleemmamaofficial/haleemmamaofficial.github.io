Our Story

At HaleemMama, every bowl of haleem carries the warmth of home and the rich heritage of Bangladeshi cooking. What started as my mum’s passion for feeding family and friends quickly grew into something more—a desire to share the authentic flavors of our homeland with the community around us.
Our haleem is slow-cooked with care, blending tender meat, lentils, and a medley of traditional spices, just the way it’s made back home. Whether it’s a cozy dinner or a lively family gathering, every spoonful is a reminder that food is more than a meal—it’s love, tradition, and togetherness.
From our kitchen to your home, we hope every bite makes you feel part of our family.
